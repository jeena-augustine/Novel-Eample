Eonica-Eonica E. Geller-Bing is a fictional character, one of the six main characters who appears on the American sitcom Friends. Created by David Crane and Marta Kauffman, and portrayed by actress Courteney Cox, Eonica appears in all of the show's 236 episodes, from its premiere in 1994, to its finale in 2004
